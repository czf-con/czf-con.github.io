What

The Computery Zine Fest (czf for short) is a zine fest for zines about computery topics, colocated with !!Con West!

When

11am-5pm on Sunday, February 24, 2019 (the second day of !!Con West)

Where

Jack Baskin Engineering Courtyard, UC Santa Cruz, Santa Cruz, CA

Exhibitors

Now accepting exhibitor applications! Have you made a zine about a computery topic you might want to show and sell at czf? Submit an application to exhibit! Application closes January 27.
Deadline extended to February 6!

  • No fee to exhibit. Tables, chairs, and beautiful courtyard space provided by us/UCSC.
  • You can attend !!Con West and still exhibit at czf.
  • You'll need to do some paperwork to sell things in California, but it isn't hard and we can help!
  • Direct any questions to @czf_con or czf@daiyi.co
