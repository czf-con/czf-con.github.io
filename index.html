✨ Computery Zine Fest ✨

The Computery Zine Fest (czf for short) is a zine fest for zines about computery topics, colocated with !!Con West! Join us on Sunday, February 24, 2019 (the second day of !!Con West) on the UC Santa Cruz campus in Santa Cruz, California.

Follow @czf_con on Twitter for updates. Or sign up for our mailing list!
